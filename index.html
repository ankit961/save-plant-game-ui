Grow Your Virtual Plant

Game Rules & Actions

Help your plant grow to its full potential by managing water, fertilizer, pest control, and protection actions. Each action has a cooldown and energy cost, so plan carefully!

  • Watering: Increases growth, requires energy. Avoid over-watering or during rain (Cooldown: 1 min).
  • Fertilizing: Boosts growth. Overuse damages the plant (Cooldown: 3 mins).
  • Pest Spray: Controls pest attacks and restores health if pests are present (Cooldown: 5 mins).
  • Mulch: Reduces water needs, protects from drought (Cooldown: 10 mins).
  • Pruning: Slightly boosts growth, but overuse reduces health (Cooldown: 7 mins).
  • Shade Protection: Provides temporary protection from hot weather, reducing health decay.
